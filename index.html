A boutique social media agency built from inside the film industry now serving all creative industries. We help filmmakers, creative brands, and entertainment businesses show up online with strategy, clarity, and a point of view.

Founded in 2023, Lost Objects began with a simple frustration: the most talented people in the creative industries stay invisible online, and the agencies meant to help them don't speak their language. Marketing a product is one thing. Representing a creative is a different specialty, one most agencies were never built for. Born out of the film world, Lost Objects was built to serve businesses and artists across the creative landscape, giving them the tools to be seen on their own terms with a team who will support and lead them to success.

We each felt like a lost object. Then we found each other.

There was a time when Kyra and Brendan each felt like a lost object. Unsure in their careers, their lives, their hearts. Then they found each other, and in creating together they understood something simple: feeling found can come from anywhere, and being lost is not the end of the story. It can be the beginning of it. That belief became Lost Objects. Because every lost object is only waiting to be seen, and so is everyone who feels like one.
