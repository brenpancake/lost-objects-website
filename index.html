Origin
Founded in West Hollywood in 2023, Lost Objects grew from a simple observation: the most talented people in the film industry were going unseen online. Kyra and Brendan Sweeney built this to change that.

The name
The name is personal. Kyra and Brendan both felt it — that specific feeling of being lost in your creative career, unseen in an industry you care deeply about. Lost Objects came from that. The belief that even when you feel like a lost object, you can still be found. And that finding others is worth building a company around.
