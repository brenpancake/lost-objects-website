Who we are

Lost Objects LLC ("Lost Objects," "we," "us," or "our") is a social media strategy and management company based in West Hollywood, Los Angeles. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or use our services.

What we collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us, including:

  • Name and email address when you submit a contact form or sign up for our free seminar
  • Phone number, if you choose to provide it
  • Information about your business, role, platforms, and goals shared through our contact forms
  • Any additional details you include in your message to us

We also automatically collect certain technical information through cookies and similar technologies, including your browser type, device information, IP address, and pages visited.

How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and communicate with you about our services
  • Deliver our free seminar and related educational content
  • Send occasional email marketing about social media strategy for the creative industry (you can unsubscribe at any time)
  • Improve our website and understand how visitors use it
  • Comply with legal obligations

Third-party services

We use the following third-party services to operate our website and business:

  • Kit (ConvertKit) — Email marketing and newsletter delivery. When you subscribe, your name and email are stored with Kit. Kit's privacy policy
  • Formspree — Contact form processing. Form submissions are routed through Formspree's servers. Formspree's privacy policy
  • Vercel — Website hosting and deployment. Vercel's privacy policy
  • Vimeo — Video hosting and playback. Vimeo's privacy policy

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

Cookies

Our website uses a small number of cookies and local storage values to remember your preferences, such as whether you've already signed up for our newsletter. We do not use third-party advertising or tracking cookies. Vimeo's embedded player may set its own cookies when a video is loaded.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Unsubscribe from our email communications at any time using the unsubscribe link in every email
  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Request that we delete your personal data
  • Withdraw your consent to data processing at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at contact@wearelostobjects.com.

Data retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law. If you request deletion, we will remove your data within 30 days.

Children's privacy

Our website and services are not directed at individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Your continued use of our website after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
