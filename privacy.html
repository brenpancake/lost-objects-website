Who we are

Lost Objects LLC ("Lost Objects," "we," "us," or "our") is a social media strategy and management company based in West Hollywood, Los Angeles. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website, use our services, or apply to our community.

What we collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us. We collect it in three places and nowhere else: our contact form, the Free Lesson popup email signup, and the Lost Objects: After Hours community application. This information may include:

  • Your name and email address
  • Portfolio or social media links you choose to share
  • Information about your business, role, platforms, and goals shared through our contact forms
  • A short bio and any other details you voluntarily include when you apply to our community or message us

We also automatically collect certain technical information through cookies and similar technologies, including your browser type, device information, IP address, and pages visited.

How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and communicate with you about our services
  • Deliver our free seminar and related educational content
  • Review applications to and operate our Lost Objects: After Hours community
  • Send occasional email marketing about social media strategy for the creative industry (you can unsubscribe at any time)
  • Improve our website and understand how visitors use it
  • Comply with legal obligations

Third-party services

We rely on a small number of third party services to operate our website and business. Each one processes only the information needed for its function:

  • Kit (formerly ConvertKit), an email marketing platform that stores your name and email when you sign up for our free seminar or our marketing email. Kit's privacy policy
  • Formspree, a form processing service that routes contact form submissions to us. Formspree's privacy policy
  • Vercel, our website hosting provider, which hosts and delivers the Site. Vercel's privacy policy
  • Discord, which hosts our Lost Objects: After Hours community and receives the information you submit when you apply to join. Discord's privacy policy

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes. You can unsubscribe from our marketing email at any time using the unsubscribe link in every message.

Our Free Lesson popup links to a seminar video hosted by Filmmakers Academy, a separate company. When you follow links to third party sites, including the seminar host, any information you provide there becomes subject to those sites' own privacy policies and terms, which Lost Objects does not control.

The After Hours community

Lost Objects operates a private, application based community called Lost Objects: After Hours, hosted on Discord. When you apply, we collect your name, email address, portfolio links, and a short bio. This application information is collected through and stored on Discord, so your use of the community is also subject to Discord's own Privacy Policy. As part of joining, applicants must read and agree to our community Server Rules.

Cookies

Our website uses a small number of cookies and local storage values to remember your preferences, such as whether you have already signed up for our newsletter. We do not use third party advertising or tracking cookies. The embedded Vimeo player used for our homepage video may set its own cookies when the video loads. Vimeo's privacy policy

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Unsubscribe from our email communications at any time using the unsubscribe link in every email
  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Request that we delete your personal data
  • Withdraw your consent to data processing at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at contact@wearelostobjects.com.

Data retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law. If you request deletion, we will remove your data within 30 days.

Children's privacy

Our website, services, and community are intended for adults 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe someone under 18 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Your continued use of our website after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
